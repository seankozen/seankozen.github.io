About Me

I am currently residing in Hashima in Gifu Prefecture in Japan, a city of 70,000 straddled by the Nagara and Kiso Rivers. Agriculture predominates the landscape on the outskirts of the city and there are areas that provide refuge for wild animals like weasels, tanuki (Japanese raccoon dogs), wild boar, and numerous bird species. Living in Hashima is an everyday source of inspiration and creativity in my work as a web developer.
